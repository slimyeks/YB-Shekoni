Alhaji Yaya Bolaji Shekoni

1919 - 1999

The best daddy Ever

Alhaji Y.B. Shekoni was a wonderful father to me and my siblings. He taught me most of the values that guide my daily lives. He lived a regimented life. He thought me values that could not be bought with worldly things. We lived in a polygamous home but the love amongst us is admirable even to some monogamous homes.
